About Frederik

I work in multiple disciplins often both as director, cameraman, sound and editor, specifically with focus on natural history and related. I have a masters degree in marine biology fom the University of Copenhagen.

Since 2012 I've worked for the Natural History Museum of Denmark on exhibition formats, webcontent and documentary films.

I'm used to work both on my own and in a larger production crew, and I also have experience in filling out different roles such as expedition planner, zodiac driver, guide etc.

I've worked in many different cultures; Mongolia, Africa, Brazil, Greenland often in remote places, both over and underwater, which has led me a good experience in working under dificult conditions.



I also have experience in arial work primarily on DJI drones; Inspire, Phantom and Mavic.



I edit in Premiere Pro or Final Cut X.
